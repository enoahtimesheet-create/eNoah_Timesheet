## ğŸ“ Submitting a Timesheet Entry

### For Work Entries:

1. **Ensure "Work Entry" is selected** (blue button at top)

2. **Enter Date Range**
   - **From Date**: Start date of work
   - **To Date**: End date of work
   - For single day: Use same date for both

3. **Fill Project Details**
   - **Project Name**: Select your project
   - **Task**: Describe what you worked on (e.g., "Development", "Testing")
   - **Task Type**: Choose Billable or Non-Billable

4. **Enter Hours & Details**
   - **Hours Spent**: Enter hours (e.g., 8, 4.5, 7)
     - Minimum: 0.5 hours
     - Maximum: 24 hours
     - Use increments of 0.5 (e.g., 1.5, 2.0, 2.5)
   - **Attendance**: Working or Work From Home
   - **Remarks**: Detailed description (minimum 10 characters)
     - Good: "Developed user authentication module and fixed login bugs"
     - Bad: "Work"

5. **Submit**
   - Click **"Submit Entry"**
   - Wait for success message
   - Form will reset automatically

### For Leave/Holiday Entries:

1. **Click "Leave / Holiday" button** at top

2. **Enter Date Range**
   - Same as work entries

3. **Fill Leave Details**
   - **Leave Type**: Select type (Sick Leave, Casual Leave, etc.)
   - **Session**: Full Day or Half Day

4. **Submit**
   - Click **"Submit Entry"**
   - Wait for confirmation

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How often should I submit entries?
**A:** Daily submission is recommended for accuracy.

### Q: Can I edit submitted entries?
**A:** No. Contact your manager if you need to correct an entry.

### Q: Can I submit entries for future dates?
**A:** No. You can only enter data for today or past dates.

### Q: How many hours can I enter per day?
**A:** Between 0.5 and 24 hours in 0.5 increments.

### Q: What if I didn't receive the OTP email?
**A:** 
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 60 seconds and click "Resend OTP"
3. Contact IT support if issue persists

### Q: Can I access this from my phone?
**A:** Yes! The application works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile.

### Q: What if I forget to submit my timesheet?
**A:** You can submit for past dates, but daily submission is best practice.

### Q: How do I know if my entry was submitted?
**A:** You'll see a green success message. You can also verify using "View Entries".

### Q: Can I submit partial hours?
**A:** Yes, in 0.5 hour increments (0.5, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0, etc.)

### Q: What should I write in Remarks?
**A:** Be specific! Describe:
- What you worked on
- Which module/feature
- What you accomplished
- Any challenges faced

**Good:** "Completed user authentication module, implemented JWT tokens, fixed login validation bugs"

**Bad:** "Coding work"
